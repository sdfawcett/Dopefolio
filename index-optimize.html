  • We offer 20 years of experience building web solutions

How We Do It

Every one of our Shopify build projects has the same four milestones:

  1. During the kickoff call we'll go over the project structure and important documents. We'll also talk about your new store's technical specifications.
  2. For the second project milestone we take a tour of the new store's admin dashboard. And we cover any additional apps or functionality.
  3. The third project milestone is a tour of the new store's front end shopping experience. We'll also do some simple theme edit training.
  4. The last project milestone is launch, where we'll hand over the keys to your new Shopify store. We'll also resolve open questions, carry out a test order, and talk about any next steps.

We do eCommerce projects primarily on the Shopify platform — things like theme customizations, store setups, platform migrations, store and sales-funnel optimizations, Shopify 2.0 upgrades, app integrations, and various types of training.
+
COVID-19 is pushing many more businesses online, and we think the Shopify platform has the most momentum and long-term potential. It’s an accessible, robust eCommerce platform that offers a lot of features out-of-the-box. It removes so much complexity for end-users, while offering powerful features to developers who build Shopify solutions.

Now that Google Shopping is funneling more users to Shopify stores than ever before, there couldn't be a better time to invest in a new Shopify store.
+
Shopify is already a top-notch platform, and they’re making it even better with Shopify 2.0. The most exciting changes for clients are all the new ways to customize your online store (with or without code). Things like sections on every page, app blocks, support for dynamic content sources, and collection filtering open up a lot of new possibilities for online store owners.

Also the new theme architecture means it’s easier for Mindful to make your Shopify store blazing fast so you get the best possible store performance.

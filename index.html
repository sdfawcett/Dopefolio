How We Do It

Every one of our Shopify build projects has the same four milestones:

  1. During the kickoff call we'll go over the project structure and important documents. We'll also talk about your new store's technical specifications.
  2. For the second project milestone we take a tour of the new store's admin dashboard. And we cover any additional apps or functionality.
  3. The third project milestone is a tour of the new store's front end shopping experience. We'll also do some simple theme edit training.
  4. The last project milestone is launch, where we'll hand over the keys to your new Shopify store. We'll also resolve open questions, carry out a test order, and talk about any next steps.
